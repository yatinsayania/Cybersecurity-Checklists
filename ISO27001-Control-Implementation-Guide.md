# ISO 27001 Control Implementation Guide

## Step 1

Identify organizational assets.

Examples:

- Servers
- Firewalls
- ERP Systems
- User Endpoints

## Step 2

Perform risk assessment.

## Step 3

Map risks to ISO controls.

## Step 4

Implement controls.

Examples:

- MFA
- Backup Policy
- Change Management
- Patch Management

## Step 5

Collect evidence.

Examples:

- Screenshots
- Audit Reports
- Configuration Exports

## Step 6

Perform annual audit.
